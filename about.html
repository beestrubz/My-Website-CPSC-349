About me

Hey there! Thanks for stopping by. I’m Brandon, a Computer Science graduate from Cal State Fullerton with a passion for building video games. I’ve always been drawn to creative expression—whether it’s playing guitar, experimenting with keys, watching animated films, or spending time outdoors. At the same time, I’ve always been fascinated by the technical side of things and often find myself asking, “How does that work?”

That curiosity led me to computer science, where I discovered my love for creating games. I’ve always enjoyed playing games, but I became even more interested in the process behind them—how art, design, and programming come together to create interactive worlds.

Since 2019, I’ve been teaching myself game development, starting with 2D pixel art games in Unity and learning through tutorials, documentation, and experimentation. Today I focus on building 3D experiences, creating assets in Blender and bringing them to life in Unreal Engine.
